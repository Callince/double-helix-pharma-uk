# Keyword Map — Double Helix Pharma UK

How to read this: pharma quality/compliance is **niche B2B**. UK monthly search volumes are small
(typically tens to low-hundreds), but intent and deal value are very high — one client can be worth
thousands. So the goal is to **own the exact terms buyers and AI assistants use**, not chase traffic.
Most of these have **low competition** (few specialist consultancies optimise for them), so they're
winnable once the site is indexed and has a few links.

Legend — **Intent:** C = commercial (ready to hire), I = informational (researching).
**Priority:** P1 = money page, do first · P2 = supporting · P3 = nice-to-have.

## 5) Where to focus first (given a brand-new domain)

1. **Lock the 7 money-page primaries** (already in your titles/meta — keep them in H1 + first paragraph).
2. **Publish the cluster pillars first** (the "Pillar" line in each cluster) — they earn the AI citations.
3. **Internally link** every cluster article up to its money page with descriptive anchor text
   (e.g. "contract QP cover" → `/contract-qp`).
4. Track rankings for the **primaries + pillars** once indexed; the long-tails follow.

> Note: live search volumes weren't pulled (the connected Ahrefs plan didn't permit it). Validate the
> primaries in Google Keyword Planner / Search Console once you have GSC data — but for this niche,
> intent and topical coverage matter more than raw volume.
