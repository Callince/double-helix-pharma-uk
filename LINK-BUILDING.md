# Link Building & Domain Authority — Double Helix Pharma UK

A worked target list to raise the domain's authority (Ahrefs DR / Moz DA). Authority follows
**referring domains** (how many reputable sites link to you) + **brand trust signals**. Work top‑down:
Tier 1 first (fast, foundational), then 2–4 over the following weeks. Consistency and patience win —
expect movement over 3–6 months, not days.

> **Prerequisite:** none of this counts until the site is **indexed in Google Search Console**. Do that first.

## Tier 5 — Relationships (the most durable links)

- **Partners & clients** — ask CMOs/CDMOs you audit, regulatory law firms, and validation/RA
  consultancies for a "partners" or testimonial link. Reciprocate where it makes sense.
- **Podcasts & webinars** — pharma‑quality shows (e.g. Qualistery GMP webinars) link guests in the
  show notes. Offer Bala as a guest on QP/GMP/inspection topics.
- **Universities / CPD** — a guest lecture or CPD session on the QP route earns a `.ac.uk` link.

## ⚠️ Never do this

- **Don't buy links, use PBNs, or "1,000 backlinks for £20" gigs.** In a YMYL niche (health/pharma)
  that invites a manual penalty — the opposite of what you want.
- **Don't spin/duplicate the same guest article** across sites. One original pitch per outlet.
- **Don't vary the NAP** — "Double Helix Pharma" vs "Double Helix Pharma UK Ltd" vs different phone
  formats dilutes trust. Pick one (the table above) and never deviate.
